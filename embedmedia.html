Instructions of Skydiving

Before th Jump

You should arrive at the dropzone at least four hours before your jump and plan to get enough sleep and eat breakfast. You should also learn some basic skills, like how to operate a parachute, land safely, and control it.

History

While the use of parachutes dates all the way back to 1100 in China, the activity we call "skydiving" can be most directly linked to France in the late 18th century.
